Welcome to my Showcase! Here you can find digital media works that I created, featuring some 3D print models and images I put together for FINE 130, a digital imaging course that I took in Spring 2017. FINE 130 images are also categorized by course modules. Click through the tabs above to filter by category. Enjoy!
Space Shuttle

Model of NASA's Space Shuttle spacecraft.


Visible: External Tank (back), Solid Rocket Boosters (sides), and Orbiter (front)

View on TinkerCAD
Seasons

A collage of the seasons interwoven in between the fence.


Featuring Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer “just around the corner”.

Afloat

A photogram consisting of bracelets, seemingly afloat underwater. The circular shape of the bracelet is reminiscent of air bubbles in water.


Varying opacities and light source in top-right corner create a sense of depth.

Carrier

The iconic opening scene from one of my favourite Star Wars films: A New Hope (Episode IV), showing a carrier ship looming overhead.


Elements are spatially arranged to create a 3D effect: the ship in the foreground, the two planets in the middleground, and the starry backdrop as the background for the scene.

Sprawl

A simplified 2D map of the Greater Toronto Area and its outskirts, using straight lines and shapes.


Notice how the roads are much denser near the waterfront and thin out as you go further away from the city centre.


Major highways are shown by orange lines and Toronto Islands represented by trianglular structure.
